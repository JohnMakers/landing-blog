Choosing the Right Grinder: 3 Things to Know

Published: 2025-08-23 · 2 min read

1) Material

Aluminum is light and durable; stainless is heavier and very long-lasting; acrylic is budget-friendly but wears faster.

2) Chambers

Two-piece is simple and compact. Three or four-piece adds a storage/pollen section and a finer grind.

3) Teeth & size

Diamond-shaped teeth give a consistent grind. Choose a diameter that fits your hand—usually 50–63mm.

Try it in person: Stop by to feel different weights and actions before buying.
