How to Clean Your Glass the Easy Way

Published: 2025-08-23 · 3 min read

Keeping glass clean helps flavor and airflow. Here’s a simple method with minimal smell:

  1. Empty any water and give the piece a warm-water rinse.
  2. Fill with isopropyl alcohol (70–91%) and add a tablespoon of coarse salt as a gentle abrasive.
  3. Cover openings with your hands or stoppers and shake for 30–60 seconds.
  4. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes, then shake again and rinse thoroughly with warm water.
  5. Air-dry on a towel. For sticky spots, repeat or use cotton swabs.

Tip: Avoid boiling water or sudden temperature changes—they can stress the glass.
