Proper Storage: Keep Herbs Fresh

Published: 2025-08-23 · 2 min read

Airtight matters

Air dries things out. Use airtight glass jars with reliable seals. Avoid frequent opening—plan small “daily use” jars.

Cool and dark

Heat and light speed up degradation. Store jars in a cool cupboard, not near windows or appliances.
